How It All Started

KiddyMart was born in 2015 from a simple idea: making school shopping fun and stress-free for families. As parents ourselves, we understood the challenges of finding quality, affordable, and exciting products for kids.

What started as a small online store selling school bags has grown into a one-stop destination for everything kids need - from adorable backpacks and colorful stationery to educational toys and comfortable school uniforms.

Today, we serve over 100,000 happy families across the country, and our mission remains the same: to bring smiles to children's faces and peace of mind to parents!
